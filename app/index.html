Application Process

Teams will need to submit an executive summary outlining their startup proposal.

Step 1. Register an individual profile at iStart (Note: This should be completed by one team leader)
Step 2. Apply at nuvc2015.istart.org for the executive summary round.

In the preliminary round, competitors will be judged on:

  • Market opportunity
  • Financial viability
  • Growth potential
  • Professionalism

Teams advancing to the semi-finals will give a ten-minute presentation followed by a ten-minute Q&A session with judges. In the finals round, teams will give a ten-minute presentation followed by a five-minute Q&A session.

F.A.Q.

Who can compete?

This competition is open to anyone with at least one Northwestern undergraduate or graduate student with a primary role within the startup.

Can I still enter the competition if I have already received outside funding?

Yes, but you must report all monetary amounts you have earned. This includes any additional funding you may have gotten after you have submitted your application.

Can I be a part of more than one team?

Yes, you can be a part of more than one team.
