F.A.Q.

Who can compete?

This competition is open to anyone with at least one Northwestern undergraduate or graduate student with a primary role within the startup.

Can I still enter the competition if I have already received outside funding?

Yes, but you must report all monetary amounts you have earned. This includes any additional funding you may have gotten after you have submitted your application.

Can I be a part of more than one team?

Yes, you can be a part of more than one team.
